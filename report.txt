PAGE: moments_mobile
DIMENSIONS: ~390x8000px (very tall fullPage)
VISIBLE:
  - Header: "מומנטים" with "Runner Runner | 2026" subtitle
  - Filter row: colored pills — type filters (multiple colors: red/green/yellow/orange)
  - Long list of moment cards. Each card has:
    * Colored circle indicator (red=danger, green=win, yellow=neutral)
    * Timestamp label (e.g. "H73", "H44")
    * Player names in Hebrew
    * Moment type label (small badge)
  - ~80+ cards visible in scroll
NAVIGATION: "← חזרה" back button in top-left
ISSUES:
  - Cards are VERY small — approx 32px tall each. Near-impossible to read on real device.
  - Text size too small for mobile (looks ~10-11px effective)
  - Filter pills may be too small to tap accurately
  - No visual hierarchy — every card looks identical regardless of importance
SUGGESTIONS:
  - Increase card height to min 56px
  - Add score/importance visual indicator (bigger badge for score>70)
  - Group by hand number or player
QUALITY: 5/10 — functional but very dense
